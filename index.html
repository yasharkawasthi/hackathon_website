About Hackathon



Hackathon is an inter-college technical competiton. It is a platform where students from around the country can present their innovative ideas helpful in making the world a better place to live in. Hackathon presents you all the opportunity to come together and work on your very own ideas and compete with other students. The ideas you create will help build a better future.

THEMES

Human
Augmentation



Human augmentation has improved the way we live, and things we do as human. How can we use this technology to make our life more easier. Any idea that is based on human augmentation and solves a problem.






COVID-19



How can the technology help us to provide a better and safe life during and after this pandemic? Have you come across this thought, then it is a chance for you. You can include ideas like how to boost our economy while facing pandemic, easy tracing of patients or any other idea you can come up with.

Education



Talking about facts, we rank 168 out of 234 countries on the basis of literacy rate. How can you help your country to improve this ranking. How technology can help to provide education to all even in rural areas? Any idea that can help in transforming or helping current education system, or anything that you can think of.

Health



It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver. With this thought we present you this theme. Any idea that can improve health statistics of our country.Topics like converting unsustainable health system to sustainable, provide faster, cheaper and more effective solutions.


Defence



This theme focuses on our country. Defence is as important as any other investment made by a nation. Defence could include any topic from Surveillance, any device that could help our forces, autonomous system etc.








Phases Overview


Hackathon is divided into three phases proceeding one after the other.

Phase 1

Abstract Submission Round

Teams have to submit their ideas on the online portal.
Projects will be judged based on novelty, significance of the problem, relevance to the theme and other factors.

Phase 2

Progress Review 1

Teams selected from Phase 1 will have to give presentation describing their idea. This will be an interactive session.

Progress Review 2

All the teams from previous round will have to present their progress in front of a panel. This will be an interactive session.

Phase 3

Final Round

Teams selected for final round have to live stream their product. There will be an interactive session between Judges and the team members.
The winning team will be decided by adding up progress review scores and final round score.

Rules & Regulations

  1. The competition is exclusively for MCA students.
  2. A team may have 1 to 5 members.
  3. A single individual may not be a part of more than one team.
  4. The project idea must fit into only one of the five themes.
  5. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  6. The project idea could be hardware based or software based.
  7. The decision of the judges is deemed final and binding.
  8. Participants need to produce College ID or any other identification document in case they win a prize.
